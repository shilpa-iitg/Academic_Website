Shilpa Singh

I am currently working as a research scholar at the Computer Generated Holography and Optical Imaging Laboratory in the Department of Physics, IIT Guwahati, India. My work focuses on both theoretical and experimental aspects of optical trapping, along with the analysis of complex imaging systems through geometrically exact ray tracing methods.

Research Interests: Optical Trapping, Computational Imaging, Aberration Analysis, Beam Shaping, Geometrical Ray Tracing.
